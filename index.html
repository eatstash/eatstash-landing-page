Create a lifelong recipe collection you actually use

Eatstash is the mobile app for people who love to cook. With Eatstash, you can (finally!) gather all your favorite recipes in one place, effortlessly meal plan with them, and follow recipes without having to touch your phone.

Your entire (!) recipe collection always at your fingertips.

Say goodbye to messy recipes and say hello to the convenience of Eatstash. Whether it's a food blog post, a printed cookbook, an Instagram video, a YouTube tutorial, a Pinterest board, a Paprika database, or a beloved family PDF cookbook, Eatstash lets you easily bring all your recipes to one place!

Super simple meal planning.

Easily put together a meal plan from your recipe collection yourself. Or reuse a meal plan from three weeks ago. Or if you're feeling decision fatigue, use Eatstash's AI-powered meal planning feature to take stress out of deciding what to cook. Tell your AI tastebud that you're feeling something, say, cozy that you've made before, and let it search your collection for you!

Hands-free cook mode

We leverage AI to give you a hands-free, voice-free way to navigate between steps in a recipe. So you can protect your phone while not having to pause your podcast! Enjoy the flow state of cooking more, with less interruptions.

Find what you're looking for

With Eatstash's robust searching and filtering, quickly find craveable options from your personally-collected recipes and add them to your weekly meal plan. You can search for specific ingredients (if you have extra of something you don't want to go to waste), or filter by what's currently in season around you, and much more.

Easy grocery list creation

Turn your meal plan into a shareable grocery list in no time.

Loving support

We're committed to helping you reap the rewards of this incredible life skill that you're developing. And we like talking to our users :) We want to build something that you'll love for years to come.
